Our Methodology

Rigorous Appraisal Standards

Every report is crafted with meticulous attention to detail, combining comprehensive local market data with internationally recognized valuation methods (TEGoVA, EVS).

I. Comparative Method

Analysis of 5-12 recent, verified transactions adjusted for specific property characteristics.

II. Income Approach

Capitalization of potential or actual rental income based on precise local yields.

Calculation of rebuild costs adjusted for depreciation, utilized for unique properties.
